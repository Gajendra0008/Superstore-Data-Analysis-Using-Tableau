# Superstore Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Tableau project analyzes retail sales performance using the Superstore dataset. The dashboard provides insights into sales trends, product performance, customer behavior, and key business KPIs to support data-driven decision-making.

The project focuses on:

* Sales performance analysis
* Product category insights
* Monthly sales trends
* Regional and country-wise sales distribution
* Business KPI tracking

---

## Tools & Technologies Used

* **Tableau** – Dashboard creation and data visualization
* **Excel / CSV Dataset** – Data source
* **Data Cleaning & Transformation** – Tableau Prep / Tableau Data Cleaning Features

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains retail business transaction details including:

* Order ID
* Product Name
* Category & Sub-Category
* Sales
* Profit
* Quantity
* Customer Information
* Country / Region
* Order Date

---

## Dashboard Features

### 1. KPI Dashboard

Tracks important business metrics such as:

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Quantity Sold

### 2. Sales by Country

Visualizes sales performance across different countries/regions.

### 3. Monthly Sales Trend

Shows month-wise sales growth and seasonal business patterns.

### 4. Product Performance Analysis

Analyzes:

* Top-selling products
* Product categories
* Revenue contribution
* Sales distribution
